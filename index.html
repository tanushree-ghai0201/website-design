About Us

1. We are a CMM Level 5 certified technology company
2. We are engaged in providing Technology to Academic institutions to convert their offline business to Online
3. eduVue is an Education Institute Management System (EIMS) which is designed on a modern, cloud based, multi-tenant architecture
4. It can be anywhere as you like, such as any geography or any devices of your choice.
5. Students, faculty and staff members of the institute now can perform all their duties and many more activities through a single window which includes web, mobile and other devices
